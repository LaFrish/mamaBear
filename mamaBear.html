Overview

Women who have experienced maternity leave at TEKsystems will provide a support structure for other women as they go through their journey of becoming working mothers. The program has three phases for the new mother and respective Mama Bear mentor.

Phase
1

Expecting Mama

Introduction into the Mama Bear program prior to maternity leave. The Mama Bear mentor:

  • Provides guidance and support prior to the expecting mama’s leave.

  • Partners with the expecting mama’s supervisor to ensure a smooth transition to maternity leave

Want a Mama Bear to support you?

Phase 2

New Mama

Post-Partum/Maternity Leave

  • Offers support to the new Mama while they are on leave.

  • Helps with preparing for the transition back to the office.

Phase 3

Working Mama

First 3-6 months back in the office

  • Mama Bear creates an operating rhythm to stay connected with the Working Mama and provide continued support.

Responsibilities of the Mama Bear:

What greater gift can you give a new mom, than the gift of time and support? As a Mama Bear you will support between 2-4 women for a 12 month period during these stages:

  • Expecting & Planning for Leave

  • Maternity Leave

  • Adjusting to Being a Working Mom

  • You will partner with the expecting/new mama, along with their supervisor you to provide guidance and support.

Are you interested in becoming a Mama Bear mentor?

What the New Mama can expect:

As a new mom in the Mama Bear program you can expect support through each stage of becoming a working mom:

  • Planning for Maternity Leave

  • Navigating Those First Weeks As A New Mom

  • Returning to Work

  • Your Mama Bear will be there to support you as a mom and as a TEKsystems employee, and help you navigate while you merge these two adventures.
